# Nexora — Punjab Client Outreach Script
### For Harpreet — WhatsApp messages to send to local businesses
### Three versions: Punjabi (Gurmukhi) · Hindi (Devanagari) · Roman (ABC)

---

## HOW TO USE THIS

1. Find businesses on Google Maps, Instagram, or JustDial in your area
2. Get their WhatsApp number from their profile
3. Send the **Opening Message** first — nothing else
4. Wait for reply, then use the right response from the script below
5. Goal of first message: get them curious, not sell them anything
