# Nexora — Punjab Client Outreach Script
### For Harpreet — WhatsApp messages to send to local businesses

---

## HOW TO USE THIS

1. Find businesses on Google Maps, Instagram, or JustDial in your area
2. Get their WhatsApp number from their profile
3. Send the **Opening Message** first — nothing else
4. Wait for reply, then use the right response from the script below
5. Goal of first message: get them curious, not sell them anything

---

## STEP 1 — OPENING MESSAGE (send this first)

**In Punjabi (Roman):**
> Sat Sri Akal paaji! Main Harpreetbol raha haan. Tuhadi [salon/clinic/gym] dekhi — bahut wadiya lagdi aa. Ik cheez puchni si — tuhade kol WhatsApp te bahut saare customer messages aunde ne? Kyunki saanu ik tool mileya hai jo automatically reply karda hai — 24/7. Free trial vee hai. Bas 10 minute lagde ne setup karan nu. Interested ho?

**In Hindi (Roman):**
> Namaste bhai! Main Harpreet bol raha hoon. Aapki [salon/clinic/gym] dekhi — bahut acchi lagti hai. Ek baat poochni thi — aapke WhatsApp pe bohot saare customer messages aate hain? Kyunki hamare paas ek tool hai jo automatically reply karta hai — 24/7. Free trial bhi hai. Sirf 10 minute mein setup ho jaata hai. Interested hain?
